Remarks:

Market Intermediaries include CCASS Participants other than Investor Participants. Negative shareholding may be occurred for individual Market Intermediaries due to special circumstances. For example, it may due to incorrect signatures being found on the share certificates deposited by a Market Intermediary upon sending for re-registration. The CCASS account of the Market Intermediary will be debited for the share quantity in question until rectification. During this period, which may take a few days, the Market Intermediary's account may show a negative shareholding.

Non-consenting Investor Participants are those Investor Participants who do not wish to disclose their shareholding.

Consenting Investor Participants are those Investor Participants who wish to disclose their shareholding.

Shareholding details of the security with multi-counters will be shown at per counter basis while total numbers of issued shares/ warrants / units of the security for each counter are total numbers of issued shares/ warrants / units of the security of all counters. The percentages of issued shares/ warrants/ units of each counter are calculated based on the total numbers of issued shares/ warrants/ units of all counters.

The total number of issued shares / warrants / units of the relevant securities / units used for calculating the percentage of shareholding may not have taken into account any change in connection with or as a result of any corporate actions of the relevant securities or the creation and redemption of units and hence, may not be up-to-date. The percentage of shareholding is for reference only. Percentage of individual Participant may not add up to the percentage of its sub-group total and total CCASS shareholding due to rounding. Please exercise caution when using the percentage presented in the CCASS Shareholding Search.
